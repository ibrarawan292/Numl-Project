    NUML Rawalpindi

    To facilitate the local aspirants with quality and higher education, on larger and wider scale at home ground, on August 19, 2020, APCOMS status was replaced with National University of Modern Languages - RWP Branch, under the banner of NUML- ISB. Another milestone was hit by offering the Faculty of English & Social Sciences; MS/MPhil programs in English Linguistics and Literature.
